About The Sound

Fusion of Cultures

Experience the unique sound that blends traditional Moroccan instruments with cutting-edge electronic production. Each set takes you on a journey through the ancient medinas and modern nightlife of Morocco.

Moroccan Electronic Fusion

Drawing inspiration from the rich musical heritage of Morocco, our sound incorporates traditional instruments like the oud, bendir, and qraqeb with pulsating house and techno beats, creating an unforgettable auditory experience.

Global Influences

Having performed across Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East, our music reflects a global sensibility while staying true to Moroccan roots, creating a unique sonic experience for every audience.
